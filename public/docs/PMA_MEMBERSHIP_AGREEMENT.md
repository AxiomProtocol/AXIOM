1. **The Association is a Private Membership Association** operating outside of public commerce under constitutional protections for freedom of association.

4. **By becoming a Member, I am voluntarily leaving the public domain** for purposes of Association activities and entering into a private contractual relationship.

5. **The protections of consumer protection statutes and regulations may not apply** to private Association transactions.

### 1.2 Constitutional Basis

I understand that this Association operates under the protections of:

- **First Amendment**: The right of the people to peaceably assemble and to freedom of association
- **Fourth Amendment**: The right to be secure in private affairs
- **Fifth Amendment**: Protection from deprivation of liberty or property without due process
- **Ninth Amendment**: The enumeration of certain rights shall not be construed to deny or disparage others retained by the people
- **Tenth Amendment**: Powers not delegated are reserved to the States or to the people
- **Fourteenth Amendment**: Equal protection and due process of law

### 1.3 Voluntary Participation

I declare that:

1. I am entering into this membership **voluntarily and of my own free will**
2. I am **not under duress, coercion, or undue influence**
