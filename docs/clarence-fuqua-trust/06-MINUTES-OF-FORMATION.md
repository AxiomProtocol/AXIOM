### 7. REGULATORY REGISTRATIONS

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to:

(a) Obtain a Federal Employer Identification Number (EIN) for the Trust from the Internal Revenue Service;

(b) Register for any required state business licenses or permits;

### 8. INSURANCE ARRANGEMENTS

(d) Obtain workers' compensation insurance as required by law;
