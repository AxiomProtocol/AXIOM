## RESOLUTION OF THE TRUSTEE

## AUTHORIZATION OF BUSINESS OPERATIONS

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Clarence Fuqua Business Trust (the "Trust") was established by Declaration of Business Trust dated ___________________; and

WHEREAS, the Trust was organized for the purpose of conducting trucking, freight transportation, logistics, and related commercial activities; and

WHEREAS, the Trustee desires to establish clear procedures and authorizations for the conduct of Trust business operations;

NOW, THEREFORE, the undersigned Trustee hereby adopts the following resolutions:

---

## PART I: BUSINESS OPERATIONS AUTHORIZATION

### Resolution 1.1 — Scope of Operations

**RESOLVED**, that the Trust is hereby authorized to engage in the following business activities:

(a) **Trucking and Transportation Services**
    - Over-the-road freight transportation
    - Local and regional delivery services
    - Expedited and time-sensitive freight
    - Specialized hauling (flatbed, refrigerated, etc.)

(b) **Logistics Services**
    - Freight coordination and management
    - Load planning and optimization
    - Warehousing and distribution support
    - Supply chain consulting

(c) **Related Activities**
    - Equipment leasing and rental
    - Maintenance and repair services
    - Driver training and safety programs
    - Such other activities as are incidental to the foregoing

---

### Resolution 1.2 — Operating Authority

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to obtain and maintain all operating authority necessary for the Trust's business, including:

| Authority/Registration | Issuing Agency |
|------------------------|----------------|
| Motor Carrier (MC) Number | FMCSA |
| USDOT Number | FMCSA |
| Operating Authority (Common/Contract Carrier) | FMCSA |
| International Registration Plan (IRP) | State DMV |
| International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) | State Tax Authority |
| Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) | UCR Board |
| State Business License | State/Local Government |
| Heavy Vehicle Use Tax (Form 2290) | IRS |

---

### Resolution 1.3 — Equipment Authorization

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to:

(a) Acquire commercial motor vehicles and trailers by purchase, lease, or financing;

(b) Maintain, repair, and service all Trust equipment;

(c) Dispose of equipment that is no longer needed or economically viable;

(d) Enter into equipment lease agreements, both as lessor and lessee;

(e) Obtain vehicle registrations, titles, and permits in the name of the Trust.

---

## PART II: CONTRACTUAL AUTHORITY

### Resolution 2.1 — Customer Contracts

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to enter into contracts with customers for transportation and logistics services, including:

(a) Rate confirmations and load agreements

(b) Broker-carrier agreements

(c) Shipper-carrier agreements

(d) Dedicated service contracts

(e) Master service agreements

The Trustee shall negotiate terms that are reasonable and in the best interests of the Trust.

---

### Resolution 2.2 — Vendor Contracts

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to enter into contracts with vendors and service providers, including:

(a) Fuel suppliers and fuel card providers

(b) Maintenance and repair facilities

(c) Insurance brokers and carriers

(d) Equipment lessors and financing companies

(e) Technology and software providers

(f) Professional service providers (legal, accounting, etc.)

(g) Factoring companies for receivables financing

---

### Resolution 2.3 — Employment and Contractor Agreements

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to:

(a) Hire employees for Trust operations;

(b) Engage independent contractor drivers and owner-operators;

(c) Execute employment agreements, contractor agreements, and related documents;

(d) Establish compensation, benefits, and performance standards;

(e) Terminate employment or contractor relationships as appropriate.

---

### Resolution 2.4 — Contract Signing Authority

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to execute contracts and agreements on behalf of the Trust, subject to the following limitations:

| Contract Type | Trustee Authority | Trust Protector Approval Required |
|---------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Standard service agreements | Full authority | No |
| Equipment purchases under $25,000 | Full authority | No |
| Equipment purchases $25,000 or more | Limited | Yes |
| Contracts over 3 years in term | Limited | Yes |
| Debt/financing over $50,000 | Limited | Yes |
| Sale of major assets | Limited | Yes |

---

## PART III: FINANCIAL OPERATIONS

### Resolution 3.1 — Banking Authority

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to:

(a) Open and maintain checking, savings, and other accounts in the name of the Trust;

(b) Deposit all Trust receipts into Trust accounts;

(c) Sign checks, drafts, and other instruments for payment;

(d) Authorize electronic funds transfers and ACH transactions;

(e) Obtain and use business credit cards and debit cards;

(f) Establish merchant accounts for payment processing;

(g) Execute banking resolutions and signature cards as required.

---

### Resolution 3.2 — Expense Authorization

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to pay from Trust funds all ordinary and necessary business expenses, including but not limited to:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Fuel | Diesel, DEF, gasoline |
| Maintenance | Repairs, tires, parts, preventive maintenance |
| Insurance | Premiums for all required coverage |
| Permits & Licenses | Registration fees, permit costs, renewals |
| Professional Services | Legal, accounting, consulting fees |
| Technology | Software subscriptions, ELD systems, communications |
| Facilities | Office rent, utilities, storage |
| Personnel | Wages, benefits, contractor payments |
| Taxes | Federal, state, and local taxes |
| Equipment | Lease payments, loan payments |

---

### Resolution 3.3 — Receivables Management

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee is hereby authorized to:

(a) Invoice customers for services rendered;

(b) Collect payments and pursue collections on delinquent accounts;

(c) Write off uncollectible accounts after reasonable collection efforts;

(d) Enter into factoring arrangements for accelerated payment of receivables;

(e) Negotiate payment terms with customers.

---

### Resolution 3.4 — Reserve Policy

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee shall maintain operating reserves as follows:

(a) Minimum operating reserve: Two (2) months of average operating expenses

(b) Equipment replacement reserve: As determined appropriate by the Trustee

(c) Tax reserve: Estimated quarterly tax obligations

The Trustee may temporarily deviate from these requirements when necessary for business operations.

---

## PART IV: COMPLIANCE AND SAFETY

### Resolution 4.1 — Safety Program

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee shall establish and maintain a safety program that includes:

(a) Driver qualification and training requirements

(b) Vehicle maintenance and inspection procedures

(c) Hours of service compliance systems

(d) Drug and alcohol testing program

(e) Accident reporting and investigation procedures

(f) Safety meetings and ongoing training

---

### Resolution 4.2 — Regulatory Compliance

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee shall ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including:

(a) Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs)

(b) Department of Transportation requirements

(c) State transportation and commercial vehicle laws

(d) Employment and labor laws

(e) Tax reporting and payment obligations

(f) Environmental regulations

---

### Resolution 4.3 — Record Retention

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee shall maintain business records for the following minimum periods:

| Record Type | Retention Period |
|-------------|------------------|
| Driver qualification files | 3 years after separation |
| Vehicle maintenance records | 1 year after disposal |
| Hours of service records | 6 months |
| Accident records | 3 years |
| Financial records | 7 years |
| Tax records | 7 years |
| Contracts | 7 years after expiration |
| Trust documents | Permanent |

---

## PART V: REPORTING AND ACCOUNTABILITY

### Resolution 5.1 — Reporting Requirements

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee shall provide the following reports:

| Report | Frequency | Recipients |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Revenue and Expense Summary | Monthly | Grantor |
| Financial Statements | Quarterly | Grantor, Trust Protector |
| Equipment Status Report | Quarterly | Grantor |
| Safety and Compliance Report | Quarterly | Grantor, Trust Protector |
| Annual Accounting | Annually | Grantor, Trust Protector, Beneficiaries |

---

### Resolution 5.2 — Performance Metrics

**RESOLVED**, that the Trustee shall track and report on the following key performance indicators:

(a) Revenue per mile/per load

(b) Operating ratio (expenses as percentage of revenue)

(c) Equipment utilization rate

(d) On-time delivery percentage

(e) Safety scores and incidents

(f) Days sales outstanding (receivables)

---
