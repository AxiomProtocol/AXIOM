### Section 3 — Acknowledgment of Duties

I understand and acknowledge that as Trustee, I owe fiduciary duties to the Trust and its beneficiaries, including:

(a) **Duty of Loyalty** — To act solely in the best interests of the Trust and its beneficiaries, and to avoid conflicts of interest or self-dealing;

(b) **Duty of Care** — To exercise reasonable care, skill, and caution in the administration of the Trust;

(c) **Duty of Prudence** — To act as a prudent person would act in managing similar business affairs;

(d) **Duty to Account** — To maintain accurate and complete records of all Trust transactions and to provide accountings as required;

(e) **Duty of Impartiality** — To treat beneficiaries fairly and impartially;

(f) **Duty to Preserve Trust Property** — To take reasonable steps to protect and preserve Trust assets;

(g) **Duty to Comply with Terms** — To administer the Trust in accordance with its terms and applicable law.

### Section 4 — Acknowledgment of Limitations

I understand that certain actions require the prior approval of the Trust Protector, including but not limited to:

(a) Extraordinary Transactions as defined in the Trust Indenture;

(b) Material amendments to Trust documents;

(c) Changes to beneficiary designations;

(d) Assumption of significant indebtedness;

(e) Termination of the Trust.
