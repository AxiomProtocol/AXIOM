## SECTION 2: DIRECTION TO TRUSTEE

I hereby direct the Trustee to:

(a) **Accept the Property** — Accept title to and possession of the above-described property as Trust property;

(b) **Hold in Trust** — Hold and administer the property in accordance with the terms of the Declaration of Business Trust and Trust Indenture;

(c) **Record Ownership** — Take all steps necessary to reflect Trust ownership of the property, including:
    - Retitling vehicles in the name of the Trust
    - Updating equipment registrations
    - Recording transfers of real property (if applicable)
    - Updating account registrations

(d) **Maintain Records** — Maintain accurate records of all Trust property and its value;

(e) **Insure Property** — Obtain and maintain appropriate insurance coverage for Trust property;

(f) **Use for Trust Purposes** — Use the property solely for the purposes set forth in the Trust documents.

## SECTION 4: FUTURE CONTRIBUTIONS

### 4.1 Additional Contributions

I reserve the right to contribute additional property to the Trust from time to time, subject to acceptance by the Trustee. Each additional contribution shall be documented in writing.

### 4.2 Business Revenue

All revenue generated by Trust operations shall be the property of the Trust and shall be deposited into Trust accounts. The Trustee is directed to treat such revenue as Trust property.

### 4.3 Contributions by Third Parties

With the consent of the Trustee, third parties may contribute property to the Trust. Such contributions shall be documented and accepted by the Trustee in writing.

## SECTION 6: INSTRUCTIONS FOR ASSET TRANSFER

### 6.1 Vehicle Transfers

For each vehicle contributed to the Trust, the Trustee shall:

(a) Complete the title transfer application with the appropriate state DMV;

(b) Obtain a new title in the name of:
    **"Clarence Fuqua Business Trust, [Trustee Name], Trustee"**

(c) Update vehicle registration;

(d) Notify insurance carriers of the ownership change;

(e) Update any lease or financing documentation if applicable.

### 6.2 Bank Accounts

For bank accounts or financial assets:

(a) Open new accounts in the Trust name; or

(b) Retitle existing accounts to reflect Trust ownership;

(c) Update authorized signatories as appropriate.

### 6.3 Contracts and Agreements

For business contracts and agreements:

(a) Notify counterparties of the assignment to the Trust;

(b) Execute assignment agreements as necessary;

(c) Obtain consent to assignment where required by contract terms.
