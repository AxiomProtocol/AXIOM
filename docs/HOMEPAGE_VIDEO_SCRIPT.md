## VISUAL NOTES FOR AI VIDEO GENERATION

### Recommended Visual Themes:
- **Color Palette:** Black, white, gold/amber (#f97316), orange accents
- **Architecture:** Modern glass buildings, smart infrastructure, clean lines
- **Tech Elements:** Blockchain nodes, flowing data streams, holographic interfaces
- **People:** Diverse professionals, families, entrepreneurs using technology
- **Tokens:** Golden circular coins with "AXM" branding

### Scene Suggestions:
1. **Opening:** Drone shot over modern city at sunset
2. **Blockchain Graphics:** Interconnected nodes, flowing transactions
3. **Banking:** Modern mobile app interface, digital payments
4. **Real Estate:** 3D building models becoming tokenized assets
5. **DePIN:** Network infrastructure, IoT sensors, data centers
6. **Governance:** Digital voting, community gathering, raised hands
7. **Closing:** Axiom logo reveal with particle effects

### Music Style:
- Inspirational, uplifting electronic
- Building crescendo toward the call-to-action
- Clean, modern, tech-forward sound

### Voice-Over Style:
- Male or female, confident and warm
- American accent preferred
- Measured pace, not rushed
- Emphasize key words: "first," "on-chain," "community," "future"
