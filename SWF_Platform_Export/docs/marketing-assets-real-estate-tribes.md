### Micro-Influencers (10K-100K followers)
**Target**: Personal finance, real estate, crypto influencers
**Content**: Authentic experience sharing their tribe joining process
**Compensation**: Free investment in Starter tier + performance bonus

### Macro-Influencers (100K+ followers)
**Target**: Established real estate educators, financial advisors
**Content**: Educational content about collaborative investing
**Compensation**: Revenue sharing from referred investments

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
**Campaign 1**: Awareness (Starter Tier Focus)
- Target: Ages 25-45, interested in investing, income $50K+
- Creative: "$100/month can build wealth" angle
- Budget: 40% of ad spend

**Campaign 2**: Conversion (All Tiers)
- Target: Lookalike audiences of existing investors
- Creative: Property portfolio performance data
- Budget: 35% of ad spend

**Campaign 3**: Retargeting
- Target: Website visitors, video watchers
- Creative: Social proof and urgency messaging
- Budget: 25% of ad spend

### Google Ads
**Search Campaign**: Real estate investment keywords
**Display Campaign**: Financial websites and real estate platforms
**YouTube Campaign**: Pre-roll on investment education content
