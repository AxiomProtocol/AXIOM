## Fee Structure

### Account Fees
- Monthly maintenance: $0 (with conditions)
- Wire transfers: $15 domestic, $35 international
- Overdraft: $25 per occurrence
- Paper statements: $2/month

### Trading Fees
- Stock trades: $0
- Options: $0.65 per contract
- Crypto: 0.5% spread
