Strategy #1: S-Corporation Election

üí∞ Potential Savings: $15,000+ annually

Recommended if combined net income exceeds $60,000-$80,000 annually

How It Works:

Instead of paying 15.3% self-employment tax on ALL income, you:

  • Pay yourself a "reasonable salary" (subject to 15.3% SE tax)
  • Take remaining profits as distributions (NO self-employment tax)

Example Calculation:

  • Total net income: $200,000
  • Reasonable salary: $100,000 (SE tax: $15,300)
  • Distributions: $100,000 (SE tax: $0)
Tax Savings: ~$15,300 per year

Action Required: File Form 2553 with IRS to elect S-Corp status
