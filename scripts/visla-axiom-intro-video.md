## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Suggested Music
- Opening: Inspiring, building anticipation (electronic ambient)
- DEX Section: Energetic, modern fintech feel
- DePIN Section: Grounded, technological, slightly warmer
- Flywheel/Closing: Triumphant, uplifting crescendo

### Visual Style
- Color palette: Deep navy/black backgrounds with amber/gold accents
- Motion: Smooth, flowing transitions symbolizing the circular economy
- Icons: Minimal, clean representations of nodes, trades, infrastructure

### Tone
- Confident but not arrogant
- Educational but not condescending
- Inspiring without being hyperbolic
- Accessible to crypto newcomers and veterans alike

### Key Messages to Emphasize
1. Real utility, not just speculation
2. Everyone benefits together
3. Easy to participate at any level
4. Building something lasting
